FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Is the initial consultation free?

Yes, we offer a complimentary 30-minute consultation to assess your needs and determine how we can best help your business. During this session, we'll discuss your financial goals and challenges, and provide initial recommendations.

Who are your services designed for?

Our services are designed for small business owners, entrepreneurs, freelancers, and self-employed professionals who want to optimize their tax situation and maintain organized financial records. We specialize in working with:

  • Digital service businesses
  • E-commerce and retail operations
  • Professional service providers
  • Real estate investors
  • Startups and growing businesses
How do I get started with your services?

You can get started by filling out the form on this page or by clicking the "Book a Call" button to schedule a consultation with our team. After your initial consultation, we'll provide a customized proposal based on your specific needs and goals.

Can I ask questions during consultations?

Absolutely! We encourage questions during your consultation and throughout our working relationship. Our goal is to help you understand your financial situation and the strategies we recommend. We believe in transparent communication and ensuring you're comfortable with all aspects of our services.

Do I need financial experience to work with you?

No experience is necessary. We work with clients at all levels of financial knowledge and customize our approach to meet your specific needs and comfort level. Our experts will explain everything in clear, simple terms and guide you through the entire process.

Can I involve business partners in the process?

We typically work one-on-one with clients to provide personalized service. However, for business partnerships or companies with multiple stakeholders, we can accommodate group consultations. If you'd like to refer a friend or business partner to our services, we're happy to arrange separate consultations for them.

Are consultation sessions recorded?

With your permission, we can record consultation calls and strategy sessions for your future reference. This helps ensure you have access to all the information and advice provided during our discussions. All recordings are kept confidential and securely stored in compliance with privacy regulations.
